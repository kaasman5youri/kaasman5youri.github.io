    Summary

    A cat sees a bowl of goldfishes and wants to catch one, which caused the cat fall into the water and drown. The writer has written this poem, so that one can learn from this story that you have to be careful when you do something.

    Analasis Ode on the Death of a Favourite Cat Drowned in a Tub of Goldfishes - Thomas Gray (1747)

    ’Twas on a lofty vase’s side,

    Where China’s gayest art had dyed

    The azure flowers that blow;

    Demurest of the tabby kind,

    The pensive Selima, reclined,

    Gazed on the lake below.

    Her conscious tail her joy declared;

    The fair round face, the snowy beard,

    The velvet of her paws,

    Her coat, that with the tortoise vies,

    Her ears of jet, and emerald eyes,

    She saw; and purred applause.

    Onomatopoeia:

    "purred"

    Still had she gazed; but ’midst the tide

    Two angel forms were seen to glide,

    The genii of the stream;

    Their scaly armour’s Tyrian hue

    Through richest purple to the view

    Betrayed a golden gleam.

    The hapless nymph with wonder saw;

    A whisker first and then a claw,

    With many an ardent wish,

    She stretched in vain to reach the prize.

    What female heart can gold despise?

    What cat’s averse to fish?

    Presumptuous maid! with looks intent

    Again she stretch’d, again she bent,

    Nor knew the gulf between.

    (Malignant Fate sat by, and smiled)

    Personification

    "(Malignant Fate sat by, and smiled)" -) Fate.

    The slippery verge her feet beguiled,

    She tumbled headlong in.

    Eight times emerging from the flood

    She mewed to every watery god,

    Onomatopoeia:

    "mewed"

    Some speedy aid to send.

    No dolphin came, no Nereid stirred;

    Nor cruel Tom, nor Susan heard;

    A Favourite has no friend!

    From hence, ye beauties, undeceived,

    Know, one false step is ne’er retrieved,

    And be with caution bold.

    Not all that tempts your wandering eyes

    And heedless hearts, is lawful prize;

    Nor all that glisters, gold.
